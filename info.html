For what it is needed?

through the this site you can separate files according to type. this may be used when you have many files and want to distribute them in various folders. if you start the following with your hand this will spent lots of time but you can save your time through the this site.

how does it work?

with the "choose file" button you should choose a file from your computer. then you have to choose the sort type. according to your wish, you must choose either sort by type or sort by extension.

through the "handling" button your files will be sent to the server and they will be handled, then you should download it. If you are registered you will be able to save your files to the server and also download it. you can access the following from here.

this will be shown in the right corner of the page with your name after registration.
